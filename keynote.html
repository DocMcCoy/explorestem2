2019 ExploreSTEM Keynote Speaker

TessVanThielen

  • Vice-President, Enterprise Wireless Product & Commercial Management
  • Rogers Telecommunications

Tess is the Vice President, Enterprise Wireless Product & Commercial Management for Rogers Communications Inc.; holding P&L accountability for the enterprise wireless business.

Tess has held many roles at Rogers over the last 13 years, focusing on product development, product management & segment marketing.

Known for executional excellence in delivering critical team projects & results; Tess’s key strengths are in planning, resourcing and directing complex activities – specifically for projects/programs that present a stiff challenge. She relishes putting plans into actions that achieve important goals.

Prior to joining Rogers in 2005, Tess lived in Australia for 8 years and worked in the telecommunications industry in product management and product development.

While in Australia, Tess completed the executive MBA program at the Australian Graduate School of Management. She lives in Toronto with her husband and 2 children.
